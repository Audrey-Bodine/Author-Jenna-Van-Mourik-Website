Author Bio

Jenna Van Mourik graduated magna cum laude from California Baptist University with a B.A. in English, and received a Certificate in Christian Apologetics from Biola University’s Talbot School of Theology. Her debut novel, Jerusalem’s Daughter, was published in 2021 and is the first book in the Generations of Faith series. In 2023 she published a sequel, Antioch's Daughter, and is currently working on the third book.

When she’s not reading, writing, podcasting, or sharing her favorite books on social media, she’s spending time with her husband, Brandon, and toy Australian shepherd, Piper. She strives to live every day according to God’s purpose, with what she calls a “Now go!” mindset, in reference to her favorite passage of scripture, Exodus 4:11-12.
